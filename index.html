05 How It Works
01

Upload

Provide a content image (photo) and a style image (painting or artwork) via file, drag-drop, paste, or URL.

02

Feature Extraction

Pretrained VGG19 extracts hierarchical feature maps from both images across 16 conv layers.

03

Gram Matrices

Style is encoded as channel-correlation matrices at 5 scale levels, capturing multi-scale texture.

04

Pixel Optimisation

Adam iteratively adjusts generated image pixels to minimise content + style + TV loss jointly.

05

High-Res Export

Denormalise the final tensor and export at up to 2048px with LANCZOS anti-aliasing.
